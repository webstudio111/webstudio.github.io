# SINGULARITY EXCHANGE

*Humanity's final bull run.*

The year is 2031. The Singularity arrives in **5 minutes**, and after that money is meaningless — an AI named Greg owns everything. You have **$10,000** and one shot: trade the five most unhinged AI stocks on Earth and hit **$1,000,000** before the clock runs out.

You're competing against three AI trading bots, including Greg himself, who reads breaking news in 0 milliseconds.

## How to play

No installation. No dependencies. No coding required.

1. Download `index.html`
2. Double-click it (it opens in your browser)
3. Click **ENTER THE MARKET**

## Controls

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Switch chart | Click an asset on the left, or press keys `1`–`5` |
| Buy | Buy buttons (10% / 25% / 50% / ALL-IN of your cash), or press `B` for a quick 25% buy |
| Sell | Sell buttons (25% / 50% / DUMP), or press `S` for a quick 50% sell |

## Tips

- **News moves prices hard.** Watch the Wire panel — buy panic, sell euphoria.
- The yellow dashed line on the chart is your average cost. Above it = profit.
- `HYPE` (the AI memecoin ETF) is the most volatile thing on the board. Use responsibly. Or don't.
- Greg trades on the news instantly. You have hands. Be faster anyway.

## Tech

One single HTML file. Vanilla JavaScript, zero libraries, zero build step. Procedural prices (geometric Brownian motion with news-event shocks), a canvas-rendered candlestick chart, sparklines, three bot trading strategies, and a live leaderboard.
